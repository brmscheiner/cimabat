Cimabat was established in 2007 by Diane and Associates.

Along with some traders, the founders’ vision was to open initially one store where you can find quality products at affordable prices. With the success recorded with the first initiative, a second Cimabat store opened its doors in 2010, at ACI 2000 Bamako.

From the start, our associates were able to offer the best customer service in the domestic market, guiding customers through projects such as house building, laying tile, plumbing, conception, and renovations of homes, kitchens and bathrooms. Our team members not only undergo a thorough training program to familiarize themselves with the products, but they also transfer this knowledge to customers willing to roll sleeves.

The key to our success at Cimabat is treating people well and we believe in our company values and commitment to create great customer service and also to build strong relationships based on trust, honesty and integrity. We do listen and respond to the needs of our customers and vendors by treating them as partners.

The founder believes in exercising good judgment by DOING THE RIGHT THING instead of just DOING THINGS RIGHT……
